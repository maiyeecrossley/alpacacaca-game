Alpacacaca

How To Play:

W - Up
A - Left
S - Down
D - Right

You have 30 seconds to help the alpaca jump across the rivers and fields to get to the other side! Use the WASD keys to guide your way around the farm!
Jump onto the logs to score points!
Each log is 100 points!

But be careful!
Jumping into the river, or getting hit by the herd of cows will take off a life, and take you back to the beginning!
If the timer runs out, then it's game over!
Good luck!
